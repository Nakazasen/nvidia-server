# ABW Non-Tech UI Quickstart

Muc tieu: dung UI de nap tai lieu va hoi chatbot, khong can CLI.

1. Mo NVIDIA UI.
2. Bam "Mo Tro ly tai lieu" de vao Focus Mode (an bot log/chat chung, uu tien man hinh tai lieu).
3. Neu can them cho de nhin, keo vach nho giua sidebar trai va man hinh chinh de doi do rong; co the bam "Thu gon" o panel Plan ben phai.
4. O panel giua man hinh "Tro ly tai lieu ABW", bam "Chon thu muc".
5. Dan duong dan thu muc project (vi du `D:\Sandbox\ten-project`) roi bam "Xac nhan".
6. Xac nhan trang thai "Da tin cay"; neu thay "Chua tin cay", bam "Tin cay thu muc nay" truoc khi nap tai lieu.
7. Tao thu muc `raw` trong project neu chua co.
8. Copy 1-3 file can hoi vao `raw`.
9. Bam "Nap tai lieu" va cho den khi het trang thai dang chay.
10. Doc ket qua: Da doc, Da bo qua, File chua ho tro, File loi doc, Ban nhap da tao, Canh bao.
11. Bam "Kiem tra tai lieu" de xem trang thai review ban nhap.
12. Neu can, nhap duong dan 1 ban nhap va bam "Danh dau ban nhap da chon" (co the fail-closed trong sprint nay).
13. Nhap cau hoi va bam "Hoi tai lieu", sau do doc Cau tra loi, Nguon, Diem tin cay, Canh bao. Neu thay "Chua tim thay thong tin dang tin cay trong tai lieu da nap.", dung ket luan va bo sung/review tai lieu truoc.

Khi nao phai dung:
- Du lieu nhay cam chua sanitize.
- Workspace sai hoac chua trusted.
- Parse error/warning nghiem trong khien ket qua khong dang tin.
