# ABW Non-Tech UI Quickstart

Muc tieu: dung UI de nap tai lieu va hoi chatbot, khong can CLI.

1. Mo NVIDIA UI.
2. Trong "Tro ly tai lieu", bam "Chon / doi thu muc" va chon thu muc project tai lieu.
3. Xac nhan trang thai "Da tin cay"; neu chua, dung dung workspace da tin cay.
4. Tao thu muc `raw` trong project neu chua co.
5. Copy 1-3 file can hoi vao `raw`.
6. Bam "Nap tai lieu" va cho den khi het trang thai dang chay.
7. Doc ket qua: Da doc, Da bo qua, File chua ho tro, File loi doc, Ban nhap da tao, Canh bao.
8. Bam "Kiem tra tai lieu" de xem trang thai review ban nhap.
9. Neu can, nhap duong dan 1 ban nhap va bam "Danh dau ban nhap da chon" (co the fail-closed trong sprint nay).
10. Nhap cau hoi va bam "Hoi tai lieu", sau do doc Cau tra loi, Nguon, Diem tin cay, Canh bao. Neu thay "Chua tim thay thong tin dang tin cay trong tai lieu da nap.", dung ket luan va bo sung/review tai lieu truoc.

Khi nao phai dung:
- Du lieu nhay cam chua sanitize.
- Workspace sai hoac chua trusted.
- Parse error/warning nghiem trong khien ket qua khong dang tin.
